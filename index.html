Momentum is a turn based, real time action roguelite where your reflexes matter as much as your strategy. Pick the perfect hand out of various weapons that synergize with unique relics to find the perfect build, and you just might make your way to the end to defeat the Lost King!

Features:-

Turn Based combat

ㅤ • Pick an attack each turn, based on attack speed, momentum cost, and multiple attack effects.

Realtime combat

ㅤ • On the enemy's turn, parry their attacks with perfect timing. On your turn, chain multiple attacks together in real time!

Unique Weapons

ㅤ • Each run, pick attacks from four different weapons, each with a different playstyle!

Synergize

ㅤ • On your runs, pick up unique relics that synergize together and with your weapon in different ways, leading to multiple strategies

Unlock New Relics

ㅤ • Unlock new relics to see them in future runs!

Just the Beginning

ㅤ • Unlock a new difficulty level each time you beat the game, introducing new features, relics, and enemy moves!
